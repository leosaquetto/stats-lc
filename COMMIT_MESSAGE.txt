feat: Add Replay feature with temporal filters and expanded views

Implements a comprehensive Replay section inspired by Spotify Wrapped,
allowing users to explore their music statistics across different time periods.

## New Components
- ReplaySection: Main component with period filters and three content sections
- ReplayModals: Three modal views for expanded artist/track/album lists
- BassPulseIcon: Animated bass pulse indicator
- UserSelectorModal: Enhanced user selection with floating animations

## Features
### Period Filters
- Today, Week (last 7 days / current week), Month, Year, All-time
- Smart dropdowns for month (only past months) and year (2024-2026)
- Dynamic text updates based on selected period

### Content Sections
1. Top Artists (10): Vertical cards with 40vh height, grain effect, rankings
2. Top Tracks (12): Horizontal paginated columns, 4 tracks per page
3. Top Albums (10): Square cards with album art and play counts

### Modals
- TopArtistsModal: Up to 20 artists with circular avatars
- TopSongsModal: Up to 30 tracks with album art
- TopAlbumsModal: Up to 15 albums in 2-column grid

## Visual Improvements
- Inline splash screen with stats.lc logo and loading bar
- New brand icons: statslc_white.svg, statslc_black.svg
- Updated favicons with transparent backgrounds
- Glass morphism effects and smooth animations
- Grain texture overlay on artist cards

## Performance Optimizations
- useCallback for handlers to prevent unnecessary re-renders
- Lazy loading with whileInView (margin: -100px)
- Memoized sorted lists and filtered data
- Limited list sizes for optimal scroll performance
